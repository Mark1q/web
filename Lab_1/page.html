The Noble Akita

Hailing from the snowy mountains of Japan, the Akita is a powerful and dignified guardian once owned only by Imperial families. They are famous for their plush, curled tails and "bear-like" faces.

While they are famously loyal—most notably the legendary Hachiko—they are also independent and cat-like in their cleanliness. An Akita doesn't just join a family; they protect it with quiet, fearless devotion.
